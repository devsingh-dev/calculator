1. Information We Collect

At SmartTools, we believe in minimizing data collection. The core functionality of our website (including our EMI, SIP, GST, and Age calculators, as well as our compressor tools) runs locally on your device (in your web browser). Therefore:

The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including IP address) is transmitted to Google. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by disabling cookies in your browser settings or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
